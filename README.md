# AstralStash - Mind Manager

Your personal space for links, notes, and ideas — beautifully organized, securely stored.
 **[Try it now →](https://astralstash.pages.dev)**

##  Features

- **Secure & Private** - Your data is encrypted and protected with your account
- **Save Everything** - Links, notes, and ideas in one beautiful place
- **Customize Your Space** - Choose your profile color and theme (dark/light)
- **Find Instantly** - Powerful search across all your content
- **Organize Easily** - Tags, collections, and smart filters
- **Works Everywhere** - Install as an app on any device (PWA)
- **Lightning Fast** - Edge-powered for instant access worldwide
- **Pin Important Items** - Keep favorites at the top
- **Export Your Data** - Download everything as JSON anytime

## Getting Started

### 1. Create Your Account
Visit [astralstash.pages.dev](https://astralstash.pages.dev) and click **"Get Started"**

- Enter your email and password (minimum 8 characters)
- Choose a name for your profile
- Click "Create account"

That's it! You're ready to start saving.

### 2. Save Your First Item

**Save a Link:**
- Click the **+** button
- Paste any URL
- We'll automatically grab the title, image, and description
- Add tags if you want
- Click "Save"

**Write a Note:**
- Click **+** → **Note**
- Choose a color
- Write in Markdown (supports **bold**, *italic*, lists, etc.)
- Add tags
- Click "Save"

**Capture an Idea:**
- Click **+** → **Idea**
- Write your thought
- Pick a color
- Click "Save"

### 3. Quick Tips

- **Paste anywhere:** Press `Ctrl+V` (or `Cmd+V`) to instantly save a URL
- **Search:** Press `/` to jump to search
- **Command palette:** Press `Ctrl+K` (or `Cmd+K`) for quick actions
- **Pin items:** Click the pin icon to keep important items at the top
- **Collections:** Organize items into collections for better organization
- **Dark mode:** Toggle in the header for comfortable viewing

## Security

- **Brute Force Protection:** Maximum 9 login attempts, then 9-hour lockout
- **Encrypted Storage:** Your data is securely stored in the cloud
- **Password Reset:** Easy recovery via email
- **No Tracking:** We don't track your activity or sell your data

## Install as App

AstralStash works as a Progressive Web App (PWA):

**On Desktop:**
- Chrome/Edge: Click the install icon in the address bar
- Or click the install button in the header

**On Mobile:**
- iOS Safari: Tap Share → Add to Home Screen
- Android Chrome: Tap menu → Install app

## Customize Your Profile

1. Click your profile icon in the header
2. Select "Profile Settings"
3. Change your name
4. Pick a profile color (6 beautiful options)
5. Update your password if needed

## Use Cases

- **Research:** Save articles, papers, and resources
- **Inspiration:** Collect design ideas, quotes, and references
- **Reading List:** Bookmark articles to read later
- **Project Notes:** Keep project ideas and documentation
- **Learning:** Save tutorials and educational content
- **Personal Wiki:** Build your knowledge base

## Need Help?

**Forgot Password?**
1. Click "Sign In"
2. Click "Forgot password?"
3. Enter your email
4. Check your email for reset link

**Account Locked?**
Wait 9 hours or contact support. This protects your account from unauthorized access.

**Export Your Data:**
1. Go to your stash
2. Click menu → Export
3. Download JSON file with all your data
